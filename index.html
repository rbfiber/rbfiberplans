Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our plans, installation and support.

You can call our sales line, visit a local office, or use the contact form on the website. Our team will guide you through plan selection, availability check and installation scheduling.

We offer a range of plans from entry-level to high-speed business tiers. Upload/download symmetry depends on the plan; please check the specific plan details or contact support for options like static IP and guaranteed upstream bandwidth.

Installation charges vary by promotional offers and location; many customers receive free installation during offers. Typical installation time is 1-3 business days after order confirmation, depending on site readiness.

For outages, call our 24/7 support line or use the support link on the site. Provide your customer ID and a brief description; our technicians will prioritize and update you until service is restored.

Yes — you can upgrade, downgrade or cancel according to the terms of your contract. Contact sales or support to request plan changes and learn about any notice periods or charges.
